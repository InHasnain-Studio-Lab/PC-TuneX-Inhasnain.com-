Privacy Policy

SpatiaX Pro App — How we handle audio processing data and protect your privacy.

Effective date: 10 August 2026  ·  Version: 1.0.1.0  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

SpatiaX Pro is a Windows desktop audio enhancement application published by Hasnain Butt Akhtar. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how that information is used, and what data (if any) leaves your device.

SpatiaX Pro is designed for local, on-device audio processing and does not sell personal data.

We are committed to protecting user privacy while providing real-time audio processing and routing features.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

SpatiaX Pro does not collect, upload, or transmit:

Personal Identity Data

Your name, home address, or personal identity documents

Ad Tracking Data

Advertising identifiers or behavior tracking profiles

Credentials

Passwords, secure vault credentials, or secret keys

Keystroke or Clipboard Data

No keylogging, clipboard scraping, or hidden input capture

Background Uploaded Audio

Audio processing is local; audio streams are not automatically uploaded to our servers

Marketing Telemetry

No third-party advertising SDK telemetry is used in the desktop app

3. Data Stored Locally

The app may write limited local files on your device only to support operation and user settings.

Runtime Settings

AppData\Roaming\SpatiaXPro\ — runtime settings such as latency preferences and endpoint-specific tuning.

Engine Recovery Journal

AppData\Roaming\SpatiaXPro\ — local recovery state used to restart audio safely after endpoint changes.

UI Preferences

AppData\Roaming\SpatiaXPro\ — local theme and interface preferences only.

4. System Access and Audio Processing

SpatiaX Pro uses Windows user-mode audio APIs for real-time processing.

User-Mode Audio Path

Audio routing and processing are performed through Windows WASAPI user-mode endpoints.

No Bundled Third-Party Drivers

The app package does not include third-party audio drivers or driver installers.

Local Device Enumeration

Audio device discovery and switching occur locally on your machine.

User-Initiated Controls

Audio mode changes, routing selections, and calibration workflows are initiated by user actions.

5. Windows Permissions Disclosure

SpatiaX Pro declares the following MSIX capability:

runFullTrust

Required for packaged Win32/WPF desktop app execution.

6. Third-Party Services

SpatiaX Pro does not integrate advertising trackers. If the app opens a website link (for support, licensing, or policy pages), that network action is user-initiated.

7. Children's Privacy

SpatiaX Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material updates to this Privacy Policy, we will revise the effective date and version number. Continued use of SpatiaX Pro after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
