Privacy Policy

SpatiaX Pro App — How we handle your data and protect your privacy.

Effective date: 1 January 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 January 2026  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

SpatiaX Pro is a Windows desktop audio-processing app. This policy explains what information the App accesses and how it is used.

SpatiaX Pro primarily processes audio locally on your device.

This policy is provided for Microsoft Store compliance and user transparency.

2. Data we access on-device

The App may access the following data locally to function:

Audio Endpoints

Audio endpoint names/IDs and state for routing and device selection.

System Volume State

Windows master volume and mute state for in-app volume sync.

Live Audio Buffers

Temporary audio buffers required for real-time DSP processing.

Local App Settings

Presets and configuration stored on your device.

License Data

Optional license/activation details that you enter.

3. Optional network and external services

Depending on features you use, the App may access network/external services for:

License Activation

Activating or validating license keys when you choose to use licensing features.

Optional Component Download

Downloading optional components such as VB-CABLE installer package.

User-Requested External Links

Opening support, policy, or purchase pages only when explicitly requested by you.

4. Data sharing and selling

We do not sell personal information.

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs in the desktop app.

5. Microphone and other permissions

SpatiaX Pro primarily uses system audio loopback/output routing.

If a feature requests microphone access (for example, calibration), it is used only for that feature workflow.

You can manage Windows permissions at any time in system settings.

6. Local storage and retention

The App stores configuration and preset data locally so your settings persist between launches.

You can remove local app data by uninstalling the app and deleting related local-data folders.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect local app data. Final security also depends on your Windows account and device security controls.

8. Children’s privacy

SpatiaX Pro is not directed to children under 13.

9. Your rights

Depending on your region (for example GDPR/CCPA), you may have rights regarding personal data. Contact us using the details below for privacy requests.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest revision.
