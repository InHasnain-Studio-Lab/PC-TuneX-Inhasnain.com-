Privacy Policy

PC VisionX is developed by Hasnain Studio X with a local‑first, privacy‑first design philosophy.

Effective date: 1 June 2026  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar / Hasnain Studio X  ·  Applies to: PC VisionX (Windows App)

1. Overview

PC VisionX is a fully local display comfort engine developed by Hasnain Studio X. It has no account, no sign-in, and no cloud. All processing happens locally on your device.

  • We do not collect personal data.
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers.
  • We do not operate servers for our apps.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize user data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

PC VisionX does not collect, store, or send any of the following:

Personal Identity Data

No name, email, phone number, address, or identifiers.

Usage Data or Analytics

No telemetry, crash analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Screen or Camera Data

No screen captures, no camera or webcam access, no microphone access.

Sensitive System Data

No passwords, tokens, credentials, browsing history, or system logs.

Tracking or Ad Identifiers

No advertising identifiers and no cross-app or cross-site tracking.

We do not run servers, so nothing can be uploaded even by accident.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

settings.json

Your display, circadian, awareness, accessibility, and privacy preferences — including any coordinates you enter for the circadian schedule.

eyehealth.json

Local screen-time and break counts, kept only to show your own insights inside the app.

theme.dat

Your chosen appearance mode and accent colour.

Local Trial or Licence State

Managed entirely by the Microsoft Store. The app reads it locally; we receive nothing.

All preference files live under %LOCALAPPDATA%\PCVisionX and can be inspected, exported, imported, or deleted at any time from the Privacy page in the app. None of these files leave your device unless you choose to export and share them yourself.

4. Network and Third-Party Services

PC VisionX uses the internet only to improve your experience, never to collect data. There are only two optional network calls.

Circadian Location Lookup

A one-off request to a public IP-geolocation service (ip-api.com) returns approximate coordinates for your network so the circadian schedule knows day from night. The result is pinned locally and never looked up again until you clear it. As with any web request, the service sees your IP address — no account or other personal data is sent. You can alternatively set your location offline by typing a country or city name, with no network call at all.

Weather-Aware Glare (Off by Default)

When enabled, the app sends only the latitude and longitude you saved to Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) to read the current cloud cover, then discards the response. No personal data is collected and nothing is stored remotely.

A network-activity indicator on the Privacy page inside the app lights only while one of these lookups is in progress. No data is ever sent to Hasnain Studio X.

5. Microsoft Store Licensing

PC VisionX may offer free trials, one-time purchases, or durable add-ons (lifetime unlocks). All licensing, payments, and transactions are handled exclusively by Microsoft.

  • We do not process payment information.
  • We do not store payment details.
  • We do not receive your Microsoft account information.

6. App Permissions

Minimum Required Only

PC VisionX requests only the minimum permissions required for core functionality. Permissions are used solely for app features and never for data collection.

File Access

Only when you choose a file — never background scanning.

Clipboard Access

Only when you paste or copy within the app.

Network Access

Only if the app needs online content — and even then, no data is sent to us.

7. Children's Privacy

PC VisionX is a general-purpose utility tool. It is not directed to children under 13 and does not collect any data from any user.

8. Policy Updates

If this policy changes, we will update the version and effective date. Any changes will remain consistent with our core principle: Your data stays on your device.
