PC TuneX - Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 July 2025
Version: 1.0.0.0
Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

PC TuneX App is a Windows desktop utility published by Hasnain Butt Akhtar. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it uses that information, and what data (if any) leaves your device.

PC TuneX does not collect or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party.

We are committed to your privacy: PC TuneX does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personally identifiable information.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

PC TuneX does not collect, upload, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location, IP address, or network topology
  • Browsing history or browser data beyond locally cached files you explicitly choose to clear
  • Passwords, credentials, or cryptographic keys stored in Windows Credential Manager
  • Wi-Fi passwords revealed by the diagnostic tool (displayed only on-screen)
  • Crash dumps, telemetry, usage analytics, or diagnostic reports to any remote server

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App may write the following files to your device, solely to support its own operation:

  • AppData\Roaming\PCtuneX\ - A JSON schedule settings file (schedule.json). Contains only scheduler configuration.
  • AppData - Temporary log output during toolkit runs. Logs are kept in memory unless you export them manually to PDF.
  • Registry - The App reads and may modify startup keys (HKCU/HKLM Run) when you manage startup programs. No data leaves your device.

4. Administrator / Elevated Permissions

PC TuneX requests administrator privileges only when required for system operations, including:

  • Creating System Restore Points
  • Modifying HKLM registry keys
  • Cleaning protected system directories
  • Querying hardware sensors and WMI classes
  • Resetting the network stack
  • Managing Windows services and user accounts

Administrator rights are used exclusively for the operations you explicitly invoke. The App does not access or modify personal files.

5. Windows Permissions Disclosure

PC TuneX declares the following MSIX capabilities:

  • runFullTrust - Required for Win32/WPF operation and elevation.
  • internetClient - Used for network speed tests only.
  • privateNetworkClientServer - Required for LAN diagnostics.
  • removableStorage - Allows scanning removable drives for disk health.

6. Third-Party Services

PC TuneX does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App uses open-source libraries (MahApps.Metro, Material Design Themes, FontAwesome.Sharp, HandyControl, QuestPDF), all of which operate entirely on-device and do not transmit data.

7. Children's Privacy

PC TuneX is intended for IT professionals and advanced Windows users. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date and version number. Continued use of PC TuneX after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
